Worksheet Exercise 4.

Show that dual maps satisfy the following properties: for any \(S,T\in \mathcal{L}(V,W)\) and \(k\in \R\text{,}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle (S+T)^* = S^*+T^*\)

  2. \(\displaystyle (kS)^* = kS^*\)

  3. \(\displaystyle (ST)^* = T^*S^*\)

In item Item 3.3.4.c, assume \(S\in \mathcal{L}(V,W)\) and \(T\in \mathcal{L}(U,V)\text{.}\) (Reminder: the notation \(ST\) is sometimes referred to as the “product” of \(S\) and \(T\text{,}\) in analogy with matrices, but actually represents the composition \(S\circ T\text{.}\))
