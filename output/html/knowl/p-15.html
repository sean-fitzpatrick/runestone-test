Let's recall what we know about vectors in \(\R^2\text{.}\) Writing \(\vv = \langle x,y\rangle\) for the vector pointing from \((0,0)\) to \((x,y)\text{,}\) we define:

  1. Addition: \(\langle x_1,y_1\rangle + \langle x_2,y_2\rangle = \langle x_1+y_1, x_2+y_2\rangle\)

  2. Scalar multiplication: \(c\langle x, y\rangle = \langle cx, cy\rangle\text{,}\) where \(c\) is a real number, or scalar.
