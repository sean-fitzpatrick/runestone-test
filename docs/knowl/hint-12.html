Hint 1.7.8.1.

For independence, consider the linear combination

\begin{equation*} w\bbm 1\amp 0\\0\amp 1\ebm + x\bbm 0\amp 1\\1\amp 0\ebm +y\bbm 1\amp 1\\0\amp 1\ebm +z\bbm 1\amp 0\\0\amp 0\ebm=\bbm 1\amp 0\\0\amp 1\ebm\text{.} \end{equation*}

Combine the left-hand side, and then equate entries of the matrices on either side to obtain a system of equations.
