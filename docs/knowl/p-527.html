By definition, we know that \(T(\vv_1+\vv_2)=T(\vv_1)+T(\vv_2)\text{.}\) For three vectors,

\begin{align*} T(\vv_1+\vv_2+\vv_3) \amp = T(\vv_1+(\vv_2+\vv_3))\\ \amp = T(\vv_1)+T(\vv_2+\vv_3)\\ \amp = T(\vv_1)+(T(\vv_2)+T(\vv_3))\\ \amp = T(\vv_1)+T(\vv_2)+T(\vv_3)\text{.} \end{align*}
