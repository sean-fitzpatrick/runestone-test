Let \(T:V\to V\) be a linear operator. For any scalar \(\lambda\text{,}\) the eigenspace \(E_\lambda(T)\) is \(T\)-invariant. Moreover, for any ordered basis \(B\) of \(V\text{,}\) the coefficient isomorphism \(C_B:V\to \R^n\) induces an isomorphism

\begin{equation*} C_B|_{E_\lambda(T)}:E_\lambda(T)\to E_{\lambda}(M_B(T))\text{.} \end{equation*}
