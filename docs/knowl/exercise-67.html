Exercise 3.1.11.

    If \(\uu\) is orthogonal to \(\vv\) and \(\vv\) is orthogonal to \(\ww\text{,}\) then \(\uu\) is orthoginal to \(\ww\text{.}\)

  • True.

  • Consider \(\uu=(1,0,0)\text{,}\) \(\vv=(0,1,0)\text{,}\) and \(\ww=(1,0,1)\text{.}\)

  • False.

  • Consider \(\uu=(1,0,0)\text{,}\) \(\vv=(0,1,0)\text{,}\) and \(\ww=(1,0,1)\text{.}\)
